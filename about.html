About Josh

I am second-year student at Northwestern University studying Computer Science and Economics! My hometown is Wilmette, IL. I live with my parents, siblings, and 3 goldendoodles Murphy, Rosie, and Archie (pictured below).

My development experience ranges from iOS to web applications, but I always love to explore new tools and technologies. Most of my knowledge is self-taught, so I don't fear any new challenge. Currently, I am building iOS apps to fulfill my own productivity needs.

Most recently, I was part of a team designing and building interactive case studies for use in a virtual classroom environment. The "games" we built were immersive, revolved around decision making, tallied points, and followed a storyline — all with the goal of keeping students engaged. You can read more here.

Outside of the classroom, you can always find me with a camera. Currently, I photograph Northwestern sports for The Daily Northwestern, but I also love landscape photography. For my entire life, I have also performed in many musicals, some favorites being Shrek and The Addams Family
